The Ultimate Guide to Security Fencing for Lusaka Homes

April 2026 Home Protection Zambia Wide

Security is a top priority for every homeowner in Lusaka. Whether you live in a quiet suburb like Roma or a bustling area like Chilenje, having a clear and secure perimeter is essential for peace of mind.

At House Plumbing Solutions, we don't just handle pipes; we specialize in high-grade security fencing installations. In this guide, we'll explain the differences between various fencing options available in Zambia.

1. Galvanized Wire Fencing

Wire fencing (often called chain-link or diamond mesh) is the most popular choice for general perimeter marking in Lusaka. It’s affordable, durable, and provides clear visibility.

  • Best For: Large residential yards, industrial borders, and garden marking.
  • Durability: Our galvanized mesh is rust-resistant, making it perfect for Lusaka's rainy seasons.

2. Razor Wire (The Ultimate Deterrent)

If you need high-level security, razor wire is the standard across Zambia. It features sharp, steel blades designed to catch and deter any intruders.

  • Installation Options: Can be installed on top of existing brick walls or integrated into wire fences.
  • Types: We install both flat-wrap and concertina razor wire. Concertina provides a more complex 3D barrier that is virtually impossible to climb.

3. Wall-Top Security Solutions

For homes with existing perimeter walls, adding a top layer of security is often necessary. We install steel spikes and razor wire brackets that increase the effective height and difficulty of your perimeter.

Why Combine Security with Quality?

A poorly installed fence is just an invitation for trouble. We ensure every post is deeply anchored and every roll of wire is tensioned correctly to ensure maximum longevity and security.
