How Much Does a Plumber Cost in Lusaka? (2026 Price Guide)

April 2026 5 min read Lusaka, Zambia

One of the most frequent questions we receive from homeowners in areas like Kabulonga and Woodlands is: "How much will this plumbing fix cost?"

Transparency is key to trust. In this guide, we break down the localized plumbing costs for Lusaka in 2026, helping you budget for repairs and installations properly.

Standard Plumbing Repair Costs

For most residential repairs, the cost is determined by the complexity of the job and the materials required. In Lusaka, typical costs range as follows:

  • Minor Leak Repairs: ZMW 800 - ZMW 1,500 ($35 - $65 USD). This includes fixing leaking taps, minor pipe sealing, and basic valve replacements.
  • Toilet Repairs: ZMW 1,200 - ZMW 2,500. Fixing flush mechanisms, resolving phantom flushes, or replacing localized seals.
  • Blocked Drains: ZMW 1,500 - ZMW 3,500. Depending on whether it's a simple sink blockage or a main sewer line issue requiring specialized equipment.

Major Installation Prices

When it comes to new setups or major upgrades, the investment is higher but adds significant value to your property:

  • Geyser Installation: ZMW 3,000 - ZMW 6,000 (Labor only). The total cost including the unit (standard 100L-150L) typically ranges from ZMW 8,000 to ZMW 15,000 depending on the brand and pressure rating.
  • Full Bathroom Repiping: ZMW 8,000 - ZMW 20,000+. This varies drastically based on the size of the bathroom and the choice of piping (PPR vs. CPVC).

Emergency Call-out Fees

Plumbing emergencies often happen at the worst possible times—middle of the night or on public holidays. Most Lusaka plumbers, including House Plumbing Solutions, may apply an emergency response fee (ZMW 500 - ZMW 1,200) to cover fast mobilization and after-hours service.
